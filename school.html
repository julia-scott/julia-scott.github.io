Tri-Wheel Trolley

  • Task
    • In groups, design a product to improve the everyday lives of older adults.
  • Our Product
    • We designed a trolley with a tri-wheel system that enables it to roll up and down stairs with ease.
  • Process
    • Project Proposal
      • The first part of our project proposal was the situation impact statement followed by a user needs assessment. This is where we identified and described our primary, secondary and tertiary users, as well as identifying our user needs. Next we outlined our problem space, which included a little bit of background, a situation of concern chart, and our requirements and constraints.
    • Personas
      • We created three different primary user personas to help us better understand our users. Personas aid in identifying user requirements and constraints.
        See one of our personas on the right.
    • Quality Function Deployment Chart
    • Low Fidelity Prototypes
      • We made three different low fidelity prototypes and analyzed their strengths and weaknesses before settling on our final design.
      • 1. Tri-Wheel Trolley
      • 2. Pulley System
      • 3. Stair Ramp
